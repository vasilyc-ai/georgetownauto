Welcome to Georgetown Auto

Georgetown Auto is a family-owned automotive service shop that has proudly served our community in Washington DC for over 15 years.

Founded by Bobby Gonzales in 2003, our shop is built on the principles of trustworthiness and integrity. We understand that your vehicle is a significant investment, and we are dedicated to providing exceptional service that prioritizes your safety and satisfaction.

At Georgetown Auto, we take pride in our quality workmanship and excellent communication. Our experienced team is committed to ensuring that every customer leaves satisfied, whether they're coming in for routine maintenance, repairs, or specialized services. We offer a wide range of automotive solutions, including oil changes, brake repairs, tire rotations, and diagnostics, ensuring that your vehicle receives the care it deserves.

As a family-owned business, we strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where customers feel valued and understood. Our goal is not just to meet your expectations but to exceed them, fostering long-lasting relationships built on mutual trust. When you choose Georgetown Auto, you're choosing a team that truly cares about your vehicle and your experience.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Honesty

We tell you what your car actually needs. If something can wait, we'll say so. If there's a recall that covers it, we'll tell you that too, even if it means you go to the dealer instead.

Transparency

No hidden fees, no surprise charges. You get a clear estimate before any work starts, and we walk you through what we found and why it matters.

Quality

We use quality parts, take the time to do the job right, and stand behind our work with a 12-month / 12,000-mile parts and labor warranty.

Communication

We speak plain English. No jargon, just a clear explanation of what's happening with your vehicle and what your options are.

Respect

Your time is valuable. We aim to get your car done when we say it will be done, and we'll always keep you updated if anything changes.

Community

We're part of this community. We believe that a thriving local business means a stronger neighborhood for everyone.
