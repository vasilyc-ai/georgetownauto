Our Story

About Georgetown Auto

A family business built on trust, transparency, and 20+ years of doing right by our customers.

Founded 2003

More Than a Mechanic. A Neighbor You Can Trust.

Georgetown Auto Service was founded in 2003 by Bobby Gonzales with a simple belief: people deserve an auto shop they can actually trust. One that explains what's wrong, tells you what it costs upfront, and does the work right.

Over two decades later, that belief is still what drives us. We're a family-owned shop serving the Alexandria and Washington DC community — not a franchise, not a chain. Every car that comes in gets treated like it belongs to someone we know, because it usually does.

Our team has seen it all, from routine oil changes to complex diagnostics on high-end European vehicles. We invest in modern equipment and ongoing training so we can handle whatever your car needs. And if we ever find something your car doesn't need, we'll tell you that too.

We look forward to serving you — and earning a spot as your shop for life.

What We Stand For

Our Values

These aren't words on a wall. They're the standard we hold ourselves to every single day.

Honesty

We tell you what your car actually needs. If something can wait, we'll say so. If there's a recall that covers it, we'll tell you that too — even if it means you go to the dealer instead.

Transparency

No hidden fees, no surprise charges. You get a clear estimate before any work starts, and we walk you through what we found and why it matters.

Quality

We use quality parts, take the time to do the job right, and stand behind our work. Cutting corners costs everyone more in the long run.

Communication

We speak plain English. No jargon, no overwhelming you with technical terms — just a clear explanation of what's happening with your vehicle and what your options are.

Respect

Your time is valuable. We aim to get your car done when we say it will be done, and we'll always keep you updated if anything changes.

Community

We're part of this community. We sponsor local events, support our neighbors, and believe that a thriving local business means a stronger neighborhood for everyone.
