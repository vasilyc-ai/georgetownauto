What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive automotive care for all makes and models. If your car needs it, we do it.

Oil Change & Lube

Conventional, synthetic, and high-mileage oil changes. We check and top off all fluids, inspect filters, and make sure your engine is running clean between services.

Brake Repair & Replacement

Full brake system inspections, pad and shoe replacement, rotor resurfacing or replacement, brake fluid flushes, and caliper service. We keep you stopping safely.

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Check engine light, misfires, rough idling, or power loss — we run full computer diagnostics and inspect the root cause before we touch anything. No guesswork.

Tire Services

Tire rotation, balancing, mounting, and flat repair. We also help you choose the right replacement tires for your vehicle, budget, and driving needs.

Transmission Service

Fluid changes, filter replacements, and full transmission diagnostics. We handle automatic and manual transmissions on most domestic and foreign makes.

A/C & Heating

Refrigerant recharge, compressor repair, evaporator and condenser service, and heater core replacement. Stay comfortable year-round.

Suspension & Steering

Shocks, struts, control arms, tie rods, power steering service, and wheel alignment. If your ride feels off, we'll find it and fix it.

Battery & Electrical

Battery testing and replacement, alternator and starter service, charging system diagnostics, and electrical troubleshooting for warning lights and accessories.

Pre-Purchase Inspection

Thinking about buying a used car? We perform a comprehensive 50-point inspection so you know exactly what you're getting before you sign anything.

State Inspection & Emissions

Virginia state safety inspections and emissions testing performed on-site. Fast, convenient, and done right so you pass the first time.

Wheel Alignment

Proper alignment extends tire life, improves fuel economy, and keeps your car tracking straight. We use modern equipment for precise results.

Exhaust & Muffler

Muffler replacement, catalytic converter service, exhaust leak repair, and full system inspections to keep your car quiet and emissions compliant.

Not Sure What Your Car Needs?

Bring it in or give us a call. We'll diagnose the issue and give you a straight answer — no pressure.
